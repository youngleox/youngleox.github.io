We propose the Nero optimizer which applies (1) per-neuron relative updates and (2) hyperspherical constraints to neural networks. Nero has strong out-of-the-box performance across a wide range of deep learning tasks and models including image classification with convolutional neural networks, image generation with GANs, natural language understanding with transformers, and reinforcement learning with proximal policy optimization.

---
title: "Ultra-compliant liquid metal electrodes with in-plane self-healing capability for dielectric elastomer actuators"
collection: publications
permalink: /publication/electrode
date: 2013-08-06
venue: 'Applied Physics Letters'

---
We developed a new type of soft electrodes with liquid metal which enables dielectric elastomer actuator films to approach its maximum strain and stress at relatively low voltages. The self-healing property makes actuators more fault-tolerant and enables a wide spectrum of usecases ranging from artificial muscle to flexible electronics.
